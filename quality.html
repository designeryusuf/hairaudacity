Beauty Meets Excellence!

Established to give every woman a fascinating and distinguished look, Hair Audacity stocks only virgin unprocessed Cambodian, Indian, Vietnamese and Mexican hairs. With lengths from 6inches to 40inches. These hairs are usually from a single donor and are cuticle aligned. They come in super double drawn and are also double wefted, preventing it from shedding or tangling.


Every weft sold at Hair Audacity are unprocessed with no chemical whatsoever. All clients are assured of the rawness of our hair with no filters, no plastic or animal hairs added to our carefully wefted bundles of hairs.


The best hair care, during preparation and packaging for clients are a top priority. Quality control staff are always on ground to ensure each bundle is independently checked.


Hair Audacity leaves no stone unturned in ensuring quality is not compromised. A black owned business that recognized the void in the community and decided to contribute to the beauty industry via quality, exclusivity and pageantry as its competitive edge or driving force if you desire.


Available for purchase are wefts, frontals and closures of lengths 6inches to 40inches. Kindly let us know your preference in hair lengths and style. We are just a phone call away!
